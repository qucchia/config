*Fix audio on Raspberry Pi*
By default, the audio output is set to HDMI. To change it to the headphone jacks, find the volume icon > Default Sink > Built-in Audio Analog Stereo.

*Import GPG key*
Import the GPG key from a USB stick by running:
gpg --import-options restore --import private.gpg
gpg --edit-key yijods@gmail.com
Enter: trust RET 5 RET Y RET quit RET
